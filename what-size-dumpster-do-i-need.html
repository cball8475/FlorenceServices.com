What Size Dumpster Do I Need?

The complete Florence SC sizing guide — pick the right container for your project and avoid paying for a second delivery.

Home › Blog › What Size Dumpster Do I Need?

Published March 13, 2026 · By Florence SC Services

Choosing the wrong dumpster size is the most expensive mistake you can make on a cleanout or renovation project. Order too small and you're paying $200–$400 for a second delivery. Order too big and you've wasted $75–$150 on capacity you didn't need.

This guide breaks down every standard dumpster rental size available in Florence SC — with real project examples from the Pee Dee region so you can choose with confidence.

Dumpster Size Comparison Chart

Think of dumpster sizes in pickup truck loads — it's the easiest way to estimate. Each "yard" of capacity equals roughly one-third of a standard pickup bed.

Size Guide by Project Type

Garage or Single-Room Cleanout → 10 Yard

A 10-yard dumpster is the smallest standard roll-off size and handles most single-room cleanouts in Florence SC. If you're clearing out a garage, attic, or one bedroom worth of furniture and junk, this is your size. It fits in virtually any driveway, including the narrower driveways common in older Darlington SC neighborhoods near Pearl Street.

Typical cost in Florence: $250–$350

Bathroom Remodel or Flooring Removal → 15 Yard

The 15-yard is the sweet spot for medium residential projects. Ripping out a bathroom, replacing flooring in a few rooms, or doing a partial kitchen demo — a 15-yard handles the debris with room to spare. This size is popular with Hartsville SC homeowners doing upgrades on the older homes around Coker University.

Typical cost in Florence: $275–$375

Kitchen Remodel or Deck Removal → 20 Yard

The 20-yard is the most popular dumpster size in Florence County. It handles full kitchen remodels, deck tear-outs, multi-room cleanouts, and medium-scale renovation projects. If you're unsure between 15 and 20, the 20 is almost always the better call — the price difference is minimal and the extra capacity prevents overflow.

Typical cost in Florence: $300–$450

Full Home Remodel or Roofing → 30 Yard

Whole-house renovations, roofing tear-offs, and large estate cleanouts need a 30-yard. A single-layer roof tear-off on a typical Florence SC home (1,500–2,500 sq ft) fits in a 30-yard with room for other debris. If you're gutting multiple rooms or doing a full Florence SC home renovation, this is your workhorse.

Typical cost in Florence: $375–$500

New Construction or Major Demo → 40 Yard

The 40-yard is built for commercial projects, new construction sites, and large-scale demolition. It's 8 feet tall and holds roughly 12 pickup truck loads of debris. Contractors working on Florence County job sites often use 40-yard containers with swap-out scheduling — see our contractor guide for details.

Typical cost in Florence: $450–$650

Special Sizing Considerations

Heavy Debris (Concrete, Brick, Dirt)

Weight matters more than volume for heavy materials. A 10-yard dumpster filled with concrete weighs 10+ tons — well over most weight limits. For heavy debris projects, providers typically recommend a smaller container (10-yard) with a higher weight allowance rather than a larger dumpster you can't fill.

Mixed Debris Projects

Doing a renovation that produces both household junk and heavy materials? Consider splitting into two dumpsters: one standard container for general debris and one small heavy-load container for concrete or dirt. This often costs less than paying overage fees on a single container.

Driveway and Access Considerations

Your available space affects which sizes work. A 10 or 15-yard fits in most residential driveways. A 20-yard needs about 22 feet of length. 30 and 40-yard containers need the same length but stand 6–8 feet tall, so check for overhead clearance (power lines, tree branches). You'll also need at least 23 feet of vertical clearance for the delivery truck.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size dumpster do I need for a bathroom remodel?
A 10-yard dumpster handles most single-bathroom remodels in Florence SC. If you're gutting the bathroom down to studs or combining it with other rooms, go with a 15 or 20-yard.
What size dumpster for a roofing project?
Most Florence SC roofing projects need a 20 or 30-yard dumpster. A single-layer tear-off on a typical home fits in a 20-yard. Multi-layer tear-offs or larger roofs need a 30-yard.
Can I put heavy materials like concrete in any size dumpster?
You can put concrete in any size, but weight limits apply. A 10-yard loaded with concrete will hit its weight limit fast. Most providers recommend a dedicated small container (10–15 yard) for heavy debris to avoid overage fees.
