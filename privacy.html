Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect information on this site

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

When you submit a quote request or contact form on this site, we collect:

  • Name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Service details (city, dumpster size needed, project type)
  • Any additional details you include in your message

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we may collect basic analytics data including pages visited, time on site, browser type, and general geographic location (city/region level). This is collected through standard analytics tools and does not identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to:

  • Connect you with dumpster rental operators in your area who can fulfill your request
  • Follow up on your quote request by phone or email
  • Improve the site and our services

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your contact details are shared only with the local dumpster rental operator(s) we connect you with to fulfill your request.

3. SMS & Phone Outreach (B2B)

Florence SC Services also conducts outreach to local dumpster rental businesses (B2B only) via text message and phone. This outreach is directed at business owners and operators — not consumers — and is described in detail on our Partner Outreach Policy page.

All SMS messages include opt-out instructions. Recipients may reply STOP at any time to be removed from further outreach. We honor all opt-out requests immediately.

4. Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services, each subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Cloudflare — DNS, CDN, and form processing
  • Google Analytics — anonymous site usage statistics
  • Formspree — contact form submissions
  • CallRail — call tracking for inbound phone inquiries

We do not use advertising networks or sell data to data brokers.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain lead and contact form submissions for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above. We use industry-standard measures to protect submitted information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request that we delete your personal information at any time by contacting us at charlie@florencescservices.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
